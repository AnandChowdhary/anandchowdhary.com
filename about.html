About.

I study at The Mother’s International School and have co-founded a SaaS startup called ClassRebels, which is a revolutionary platform for students and teachers to connect outside the classroom.

You can drop me a line at ac@classrebels.com or find me on Facebook, Twitter, or Google+. I also write code on Github and ask questions on Quora.

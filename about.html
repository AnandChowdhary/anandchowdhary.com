About

Hey! My name is Anand Chowdhary, and I'm a product designer and consultant to over 10 startups. These days, I'm working at Oswald Foundation to build new technologies for the differently abled.

Previously, I was a consulant to companies like Cyankart, Crink Jewel, and Unifiers, and have worked with organizations like the Government of Delhi. I attended the Unitedworld Institute of Design for two months before dropping out and starting the Foundation.

I occasionally pen down my thoughts on my blog and have a very outdated portfolio. You can send me an email at hello on this domain (but I'm currently not accepting any new projects.) You can also connect with me on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook. I write code on Github and words on Medium.

Thanks for visiting!

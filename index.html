About

Press

Hello! My name is Anand Chowdhary and I’m an interdisciplinary designer and user experience consultant to over 10 startups. I’m 18-years-old and currently studying at The Mother's International School.

I founded Talking Teal, an international design consultancy company that helps transform businesses into brands that people connect with, and Class Rebels, an education technology company that breaks classroom barriers.

I love writing and talking about “experience design”, which combines what people think and feel when they interact with daily objects, and uses them as digital and print design principles to deliver great experiences. I enjoy listening to The Beatles, Vanessa-Mae, and Jim Reeves. I also contribute to the open-source community.
